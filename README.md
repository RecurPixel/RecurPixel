## 💡 Architecture Philosophy

I believe in:

- ✅ **Clean Architecture** - Separation of concerns, dependency inversion
- ✅ **CQRS** - Read/write optimization for complex domains
- ✅ **Event Sourcing** - Complete audit trails for critical data
- ✅ **DDD** - Rich domain models that speak business language
- ✅ **Testing** - Unit tests, integration tests, benchmarking
- ✅ **Performance** - Measured optimization, not premature

> Make it work → Make it clean → Make it fast
> Use whatever gets the job done — AI, Stack Overflow, official docs, or a blog post written by someone's grandma. The best engineers aren't the ones who know everything, they're the ones who know how to find anything.
